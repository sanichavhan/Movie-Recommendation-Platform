# Movie Recommendation System - User Guide

## Getting Started

### Searching for Movies
1. **Navigate to Search Page** - Click on the search icon in the navigation bar
2. **Type Your Query** - Enter a movie name, genre, or actor name
3. **View Results** - Movies matching your search appear instantly
4. **Recent Searches** - Your last 5 searches are saved for quick access

### Example Searches
- Movie names: "Avengers", "Inception", "The Matrix"
- Genres: "Action", "Comedy", "Horror"
- Actor names: "Tom Hanks", "Scarlett Johansson"
- Multi-word: "superhero action"

---

## Finding Similar Movies

### Method 1: From Search Results
1. **Search for a Movie** - Use the search bar
2. **Click on a Movie Card** - Click on any movie in the results
3. **View Recommendations Modal** - A popup appears showing:
   - The selected movie's poster and details
   - Rating and release year
   - Movie description
   - **Up to 6 similar movies** below
4. **Explore Further**:
   - Click "View Full Details" to see complete information
   - Click on any similar movie to view its recommendations
   - Close the modal by clicking the ✕ button

### Method 2: From Movie Details Page
1. **Navigate to Movie Details** - Click on any movie card
2. **Scroll Down** - Look for "Similar Movies" section
3. **Browse Recommendations** - View similar movies in a carousel
4. **Click to Explore** - Click on any recommended movie to see its details

## Understanding the Recommendation Modal

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ ✕                                           │
│ ┌─────────┐                                 │
│ │ Poster  │ Movie Title                     │
│ │         │ ⭐ 8.5  📅 2023  🕒 148 min     │
│ │         │ Brief description...             │
│ │         │ [View Full Details Button]      │
│ └─────────┘                                 │
│                                              │
│ Similar Movies You Might Like               │
│ ┌──────┐ ┌──────┐ ┌──────┐                 │
│ │Movie1│ │Movie2│ │Movie3│ ...             │
│ └──────┘ └──────┘ └──────┘                 │
│                                              │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### What Each Section Means
- **Movie Poster** - Visual representation of the selected movie
- **Title & Rating** - Movie name and audience rating (1-10)
- **Release Year** - Year the movie was released
- **Duration** - How long the movie is (in minutes)
- **Description** - Brief plot summary
- **Similar Movies** - Movies based on genre, theme, and content

## Tips for Better Recommendations

### 1. Search by Specific Criteria
- **Genre-focused**: Search "sci-fi action" for action science fiction movies
- **Actor-focused**: Search actor names to find their movies
- **Theme-based**: Search themes like "superhero", "heist", "horror"

### 2. Explore Different Movies
- Start with one movie you like
- Look at its recommendations
- Click on a recommended movie and explore its recommendations
- Build a chain of discovery

### 3. Understanding Why Movies Are Similar
Movies are recommended based on:
- Same genres (Action, Drama, Comedy, etc.)
- Similar plots and themes
- Comparable IMDB/audience ratings
- Production era and style

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: No Results Found
**Solution:**
- Check spelling of the movie name
- Try searching by actor name instead
- Try a more general search (e.g., just the genre)
- Search for a different movie

### Issue: Similar Movies Not Loading
**Solution:**
- Move mouse away and back to the movie card
- Close and reopen the recommendation modal
- Try clicking a different movie
- Check your internet connection

### Issue: Images Not Showing
**Solution:**
- Refresh the page (F5)
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check internet connection

### Issue: Search Takes Too Long
**Solution:**
- Check internet speed
- Try a simpler search query
- Wait for the current search to complete
- Refresh the page

## FAQs

**Q: Why can't I find a specific movie?**
A: The movie might be very new or not available in TMDB. Try searching for it on TMDB.org directly.

**Q: Are recommendations accurate?**
A: Recommendations are AI-generated from TMDB using genre, plot, and rating similarity. Results may vary.

**Q: Can I save my favorite searches?**
A: Yes! Your last 5 searches are automatically saved and appear when you visit the search page.

**Q: What if an image doesn't load?**
A: A placeholder image appears. Refresh the page or check your internet connection.

**Q: How often is the movie data updated?**
A: The data comes from TMDB and is updated daily.

**Q: Can I use this on mobile?**
A: Yes! The site is fully responsive and works on all devices.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes, your searches are stored locally on your device only.
